About

With over 15 years experience within the industry, RooBoo can handle jobs ranging from SME's with a fleet of vehicles to tradesman with a single van.

RooBoo was created by Chris Berry, a recognized name within the industry.
The idea was to create a company that is able to provide a fast, professional service with competitive prices.

RooBoo covers all of a customers needs with concern to customizing their van.
With plylining, custom interiors, shelving, racking, roof racks and bars.
For those concerned with security, RooBoo also provides a number of options concerning dead-locks and rep-locks.
Whatever the need you can count on the Berry to both bust your bulkhead and roo your boo with equal enthusiasm.
